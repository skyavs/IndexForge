Why Choose IndexForge?

For Asset Managers

Build, test and launch thematic indices in a fraction of the time — no engineers required. Ideal for ETFs, certificates, and structured notes. Launch your smart beta strategy in days, not months.

For Investment Advisors

Design custom benchmarks aligned with your clients' goals. Deliver full transparency, analytics and compliance-ready reporting. Offer tailor-made index solutions to high-net-worth clients.

For Fintech Builders

Validate your index idea quickly before building full-scale products. Iterate fast with real data and a working prototype. Perfect for pitch decks, MVPs, and investor demos.

For Researchers & Analysts

Backtest strategies with full control over components, weights, and calculation methods. No coding. No infrastructure setup. Turn your Excel models into real-time indices.

Built on Proven Tech Stack

Built on Supabase (PostgreSQL + Edge), Vercel (serverless), and Yahoo Finance API. Runs daily with secure API access — lightweight, no-code-ready, and scalable by design.

  • Powered by Supabase (PostgreSQL + Edge Functions), Vercel (serverless infrastructure), and Yahoo Finance (market data via API)
  • Daily automated updates via Vercel Cron
  • Secure API with key-based access
  • Frontend built with Next.js + Tailwind — no-code-friendly, lightweight, and production-ready
